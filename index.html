Motivation

Why am I doing this?

I started this project last year when I'd had enough of RPI's sites not serving me well. It has been a consistent struggle for me since freshman year to find any valid information online. I always ran into dead links, outdated information, and just overall nothing useful. I came out with more questions than when I started looking. I always ended up incredibly frustrated and stuck in my future plans with RPI. So I decided to take matters into my own hands and help others in the same boat!

Purpose

What is the point of this site?

This FAQ site is not intended to replace RPI's vast network of sites in any way. It's just meant to supplement them in a much easier to navigate format and streamline the most important information. As it grows, I would definitely be looking to expand the scope of the site a bit but not to the effect of replicating the entire RPI sites.
